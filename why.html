Why Waste Is Everyone’s Problem?

What’s the Big Deal?

Waste doesn’t disappear — it just moves out of sight.

Landfills, oceans, air: it’s piling up, poisoning ecosystems and communities.

Only 9% of all plastic ever made has been recycled.

Waste is Unequal

Poorer communities suffer more from toxic waste exposure.

Fast fashion and e-waste pollute countries that didn’t even produce them.

Why Youth? Why Now?

We’re the most aware generation.

We have creativity, digital tools, and the voice to push change.

Every wrapper, every leftover, every “it’s just one plastic” moment adds up.
Let’s rethink the mess we leave behind.
